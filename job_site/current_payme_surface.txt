=== INVOICE BUILDERS (payme-admin-minimal/modules/light-crm/lib/invoiceBuilders.js) ===
buildInvoiceWireText({ company, customer, invoiceNumber, amount, items, generatedLink })
  — plain-text invoice format; outputs: company header, bill-to, items list, total, pay link

buildInvoiceHtml({ company, customer, invoiceNumber, amount, items, generatedLink, createdAt })
  — HTML email invoice with: company logo, company name, invoice#, date, total, bill-to block, line items table, pay link
  — suitable for browser print-to-PDF

computeInvoiceTotal(items)   — sum of qty × unit for all line items
normalizeInvoiceItems(items) — validates and filters item entries
buildMailtoHref({ emailTo, subject, body }) — mailto: link builder
buildTxUrl(txHash, chainId) — blockchain tx URL (Base: basescan.org, Eth: etherscan.io)
downloadTextFile({ filename, content, mime }) — client-side file download trigger
formatMoney(n)               — USDC amount formatter (removes trailing zeros)

=== PDF GENERATION PATH ===
No dedicated PDF library (no jsPDF, puppeteer, or similar).
Current PDF path: buildInvoiceHtml() → browser print-to-PDF (manual user action).
downloadTextFile() generates .txt wire-text download.
No server-side PDF generation endpoint found.
