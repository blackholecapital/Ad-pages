=== AUTH GATE: ADMIN AND STUDIO ===
Gate component: RequireGate
File: apps/product-shell/src/components/gate/RequireGate.tsx

Mechanism:
- RequireGate wraps admin and studio routes as a layout route in router.tsx
- Unauthenticated visitors are shown LoginModal (title: "Access Gate") instead of the page content
- Access is granted when: (walletConnected && signedMessage) OR demoBypass === true
- On access granted: <Outlet /> renders the requested child route (AdminPage or StudioPage)

=== GATED ROUTES (router.tsx lines 64-73) ===
All routes under RequireGate layout — unauthenticated requests show LoginModal:

  /admin                              → AdminPage
  /:slug/admin                        → AdminPage
  /:designation/:slug/admin           → AdminPage
  /:slug/studio                       → StudioPage
  /:designation/:slug/studio          → StudioPage

=== AUTH ENTRY TRIGGER ===
Nav item "Admin" in TopNav links to /:slug/admin.
Unauthenticated click → RequireGate intercepts → LoginModal rendered.
LoginModal presents: Connect Wallet button, Sign Message button, Demo Bypass toggle.
Authenticated (wallet + signed OR demo bypass) → AdminPage rendered.
